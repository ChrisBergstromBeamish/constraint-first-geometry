
**Suggested corpus order for a referee, by goal:**

- **To judge the strongest banked claim first:** start at **Paper 1 (GUT)** — it is the only
  paper making a *certificate* claim (scoped-GUT Gates 1–10). It is the load-bearing one.
- **To judge honesty/discipline:** start at the **TOE_FINAL capstone §V truth table**, then its
  STRONGEST OBJECTIONS section. The capstone is where every gap is dispositioned in exactly one
  terminal state with its falsifier attached.
- **To judge the least-over-claimed material:** **Paper 2 (Forces)** is an interface/consistency
  paper that re-closes no gate; **Paper 3 (Quantum)** prints its own weakest link (Σ = AUDIT) on
  the cover.
- **Authoritative tie-breaker rule:** where the manuscripts conflict, `TOE_FINAL_merged.md`
  (newest ledger) wins (`SOT_DESIGNATION_2026-06-12.md` §3). One known within-corpus lag is
  logged: `Fable_Quantum §15` carries a stale S-7 status vs the capstone's Gap-04 closure.
