## 8.2 The selector discipline: the seven standing rules

1. **Current geometry first.** No extension is considered unless the current geometry *structurally fails* the requirement at hand. A want is not a failure.

5. **No silent promotions** — of BG-10, of Λ, of anything — under any framing. (The preservation rulings of the truth table are this rule, applied.)
